Ke hoach cac giai doan hoan thien Assignment RAG (Pittsburgh + CMU)

Giai doan 1 - Hieu de bai va thiet ke pham vi
Muc tieu:
- Chot yeu cau ky thuat, policy model/data, metric danh gia.
Cong viec chinh:
- Doc ky de bai va rubric.
- Chot pham vi cau hoi: history, culture, trivia, upcoming events.
- Chon stack co ban: embedder + retriever + reader.
Dau ra:
- 1 tai lieu requirement + architecture draft.
Tieu chi hoan thanh:
- Toan nhom thong nhat pipeline va ke hoach thuc hien.

Giai doan 2 - Thu thap knowledge resource
Muc tieu:
- Tao kho tai lieu cong khai lien quan Pittsburgh/CMU.
Cong viec chinh:
- Crawl/scrape cac website duoc goi y (Wikipedia, city pages, CMU, events, food, sports, culture).
- Thu thap them cac subpage va PDF lien quan.
- Luu metadata: URL, ngay crawl, loai tai lieu.
Dau ra:
- Thu muc raw_data/ gom HTML/PDF/TXT + metadata.csv.
Tieu chi hoan thanh:
- Bao phu du chu de va co truy vet nguon ro rang.

Giai doan 3 - Tien xu ly va chuan hoa du lieu
Muc tieu:
- Chuyen raw data thanh corpus sach cho retrieval.
Cong viec chinh:
- Parse HTML bang BeautifulSoup, parse PDF bang pypdf/pdfplumber.
- Loai bo boilerplate, menu, script, noise.
- Chunk van ban (theo doan/so tu), gan source id.
Dau ra:
- Corpus da lam sach + file chunks co schema on dinh.
Tieu chi hoan thanh:
- Van ban de truy hoi, chunk chat luong, co lien ket nguon.

Giai doan 4 - Annotate data test/train
Muc tieu:
- Tao tap QA cho phat trien he thong.
Cong viec chinh:
- Tao data/test/questions.txt va reference_answers.txt da dang.
- Tao data/train/questions.txt va reference_answers.txt (manual hoac bo sung tu external data hop le).
- Quy uoc dap an ngan gon, co the co nhieu reference cach nhau boi ';'.
Dau ra:
- Bo train/test theo dung format nop bai.
Tieu chi hoan thanh:
- Tap test du lon, da dang loai cau hoi, annotation ro rang.

Giai doan 5 - Danh gia chat luong annotation (IAA)
Muc tieu:
- Chung minh do tin cay cua du lieu gan nhan.
Cong viec chinh:
- It nhat 2 thanh vien annotate trung mot tap con random cua test.
- Tinh IAA (vi du exact match agreement/F1 agreement tuy quy uoc nhom).
- Ghi nhan bat dong va quy tac resolve.
Dau ra:
- Bao cao IAA + phuong phap + ket qua.
Tieu chi hoan thanh:
- Co so lieu IAA va quy trinh thong nhat nhan.

Giai doan 6 - Xay dung va lap rap RAG
Muc tieu:
- Hoan thanh pipeline QA retrieval-augmented.
Cong viec chinh:
- Index chunks vao vector DB (vd: Chroma/FAISS).
- Xay retriever (top-k, similarity, rerank neu co).
- Xay reader/generator (LLM open qua HuggingFace) va prompt template.
- Tao baseline closed-book de so sanh.
Dau ra:
- Ma nguon chay duoc end-to-end tren tap test noi bo.
Tieu chi hoan thanh:
- He thong sinh duoc cau tra loi dung dinh dang 1 dong/1 cau hoi.

Giai doan 7 - Thi nghiem, danh gia, phan tich
Muc tieu:
- Chon cau hinh tot nhat va rut ra ket luan ky thuat.
Cong viec chinh:
- Thu it nhat 2 bien the he thong (khac model/retriever/prompt).
- Tinh Recall, EM, F1 tren test noi bo.
- Phan tich theo nhom cau hoi (history/events/temporal/etc.).
- So sanh RAG vs closed-book va trinh bay vi du output.
Dau ra:
- Bang ket qua + phan tich dinh luong/dinh tinh.
Tieu chi hoan thanh:
- Co bang chung thuyet phuc cho lua chon he thong cuoi.

Giai doan 8 - Chay tren test private va tao output nop bai
Muc tieu:
- Tao file du doan cho de private cua mon hoc.
Cong viec chinh:
- Chay he thong da chot tren questions private.
- Tao system_output_1.txt (toi da them 2 bien the neu can).
- Kiem tra format dong va thu tu cau tra loi.
Dau ra:
- system_outputs/system_output_{1,2,3}.txt
Tieu chi hoan thanh:
- File hop le va san sang nop.

Giai doan 9 - Hoan tat bao cao va dong goi submission
Muc tieu:
- Hoan chinh tat ca deliverables theo rubric.
Cong viec chinh:
- Viet report (data creation, model details, results, analysis).
- Cap nhat GitHub repo + README huong dan chay.
- Tao contributions.md (chia cong viec tung thanh vien).
- Dong goi zip dung cau truc ANDREWID/.
Dau ra:
- Goi nop hoan chinh tren Canvas.
Tieu chi hoan thanh:
- Du file, dung format, noi dung dat tieu chi cham diem.

Checklist nhanh truoc khi nop
- Co data train/test do nhom tao.
- Co IAA va mo ta cach tinh.
- Co it nhat 2 bien the mo hinh de so sanh.
- Co ket qua EM/F1/Recall tren test noi bo.
- Co output tren private test.
- Co report + github_url.txt + contributions.md + README.md.
